## Was sie machen
Plurai offers vibe training for AI agent reliability. Users describe what their agent should and should not do, and Plurai automatically generates training data, validates it, and deploys a custom evaluation model in minutes. The experience is analogous to vibe coding, but applied to evaluation and guardrails. Under the hood, small language models deliver sub-100ms latency, 8x lower cost than GPT-as-judge, and over 43% fewer failures. Monitoring is always-on rather than sampled. The technology is built on published research (BARRED) and requires no labeled data, no annotation pipeline, and no prompt engineering from the user. Plurai also provides an open-source component via GitHub (intellagent).
