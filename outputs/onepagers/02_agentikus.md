## Was sie machen
Agentikus is building a control interface for local AI agents. The platform enables operators to coordinate agents, keep context close, and review work from a single shared surface. It introduces core concepts such as workspaces, sessions, conversations, projects, files, and context to help businesses build and manage AI-native workflows. The product is positioned as an infrastructure layer for companies looking to thrive in the AI age.
