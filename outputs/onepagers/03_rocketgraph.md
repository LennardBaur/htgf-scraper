## In einem Satz
AI-powered log analysis that automatically clusters, summarises, and triages system health issues so engineers never have to read logs manually.

## Was sie machen
RocketGraph is a developer-focused observability tool with the tagline "Never look at logs again." It ingests log data (sourced from ClickHouse in the observed demo) and uses large language models (Claude / GPT-4o) to automatically cluster log events, flag anomalies, generate plain-English summaries of system health, and recommend concrete remediation actions. The product surfaces degraded system states, SLA breaches, and inventory/operational issues without requiring engineers to manually trawl through raw logs. It also includes an on-call wake decision layer, indicating integration with incident-management workflows.
