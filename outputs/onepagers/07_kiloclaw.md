## Was sie machen
KiloClaw is a fully managed, hosted version of OpenClaw, the most popular open source AI agent platform. Rather than requiring users to self-host on local hardware (e.g. a Mac mini), KiloClaw handles all infrastructure, security, updates, and monitoring, allowing teams to focus on what their agent actually does rather than keeping it alive. The company also offers PinchBench, a benchmarking tool to help users find the best AI model for their OpenClaw deployment.
