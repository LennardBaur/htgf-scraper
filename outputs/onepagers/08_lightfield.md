## Was sie machen
Lightfield is an AI-native CRM that automatically builds and maintains itself by reading your emails, meetings, and calls — eliminating manual data entry. Users can connect their inbox or import a spreadsheet/CSV from an existing CRM and have everything recreated in under five minutes. The product allows users to query their CRM in plain English (e.g. who needs follow-up, what objections keep coming up, how the ICP has shifted) based on actual conversation data. It also takes action by drafting follow-ups, creating board decks, building proposals, and more.
