## In einem Satz
Evals, closed control loop, and auto-rollback for production AI agents.

## Was sie machen
TruLayer is an AI reliability platform purpose-built for teams shipping LLM features to production. It provides distributed tracing, automated evaluation (25 pre-built evaluators), and a closed control loop that retries with fallback models, gates high-stakes actions on human approval, and rolls back automatically when a fix introduces a new regression. Unlike general-purpose APM tools, TruLayer unifies tracing, automated evaluation, and human feedback in a single pipeline — turning every production failure into a system fix before it hits the next user. It is OTEL-native, integrates with OpenAI, Anthropic, LangChain, LangGraph, AutoGen, CrewAI, LlamaIndex, and more, and can be instrumented in under five minutes with two lines of code. A free tier includes 1M spans/month with no credit card required.
