## In einem Satz
A living, browsable knowledge graph that helps developers and researchers find and compare AI language models by use case, capability, and benchmark.

## Was sie machen
ThinkLLM is a continuously updated knowledge graph about AI models, designed to help developers, researchers, and AI-curious users build practical intuition about the LLM landscape. The platform covers thousands of open-weight and commercial API models, organised across use cases (e.g. agentic tasks, coding, RAG, customer support) and capabilities (e.g. reasoning, multilingual, multimodal, tool use). AI agents continuously discover new models, gather metadata from publicly available sources such as HuggingFace Hub, the Open LLM Leaderboard, Chatbot Arena, and provider RSS feeds, and synthesise original descriptions and capability scores. Every entry is reviewed before publication. The site also aggregates benchmark scores, human preference rankings, pricing data, and research papers, giving users a single place to evaluate and compare models for their specific needs.
