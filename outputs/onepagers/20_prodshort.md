## In einem Satz
Turn meetings into ready-to-post shorts and posts automatically — no scripts, no fake AI voice.

## Was sie machen
ProdShort captures the content founders already produce in meetings and automatically transforms it into ready-to-post short videos, LinkedIn posts, and tweets. Unlike generic AI content generators, ProdShort does not fabricate content — it records, cuts, and refines what users actually say, preserving their authentic voice. The tool removes the time burden of content creation for founders by eliminating the need for scripts, manual editing, or post-meeting writing sessions.
