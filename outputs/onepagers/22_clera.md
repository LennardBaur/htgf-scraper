## Was sie machen
Clera is an AI talent agent that helps job seekers find roles they actually want. It gets to know candidates' preferences through conversations over iMessage and WhatsApp, surfaces relevant job opportunities, and makes direct introductions to companies — eliminating the need to spend hours searching and applying for jobs.
