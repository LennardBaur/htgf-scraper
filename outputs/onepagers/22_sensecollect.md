## In einem Satz
Self-service web data extraction tool that lets non-technical teams scrape public web data and download structured CSV exports in minutes.

## Was sie machen
SenseCollect is a no-code, self-service web scraping platform designed for non-technical teams who need structured public web data for lead generation, research, and market intelligence. Users choose a pre-built scraping tool (currently Google Maps Leads, with Instagram, Amazon, and TikTok exporters coming soon), enter a plain-language search query, and receive a clean CSV download when the job completes. The platform operates on a pay-as-you-go credit model with one-time credit packs ($19 for 100 credits, $49 for 300, $99 for 800); credits never expire and failed jobs are automatically refunded. SenseCollect handles all scraping infrastructure, job tracking, result storage, and download preparation, removing the need for Apify accounts, browser extensions, or custom scraper configuration.
