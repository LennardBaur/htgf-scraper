## Was sie machen
ForwardPass Technology, run by Alister Palmer, publishes "The Weekly AI Update for Decision Makers" — a curated newsletter covering key AI news, advancements, courses, and content designed to be digestible in under 5 minutes, topped with an original cartoon. Beyond the newsletter, ForwardPass has developed an experimental MCP (Model Context Protocol) server that allows subscribers to connect the newsletter to their AI assistant of choice, enabling personalised delivery times and frequencies independent of the author's publication schedule. ForwardPass Technology also offers AI workshops for executive teams in Australia. Alister Palmer brings 18 years of industry experience spanning Capital Markets, enterprise AI, and holds a Masters of Data Science.
