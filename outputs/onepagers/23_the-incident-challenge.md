## In einem Satz
A bi-weekly timed challenge where developers diagnose and fix real-world production incidents to win cash prizes.

## Was sie machen
The Incident Challenge is a competitive, bi-weekly online platform for developers. Every second Monday, a new production incident scenario is released for 24 hours. Participants are given logs, architecture diagrams, code, documentation, and clues, and must determine what broke, why it broke, and how to fix it. Speed and correctness both matter — the fastest participant with the correct answer wins a cash prize. The platform targets skilled developers who enjoy debugging complex, messy systems and finding signal in noise.
