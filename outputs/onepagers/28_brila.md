## In einem Satz
One-page websites automatically generated from real Google Maps reviews using AI and Jobs to Be Done analysis.

## Was sie machen
Brila is an AI-powered website generator that takes a content-first approach to building one-page websites for local businesses. Instead of providing generic templates with placeholder copy, Brila reads a business's real Google Maps reviews, applies a Jobs to Be Done framework to identify why customers actually choose that business, and then builds a one-page website using real patterns, real customer wording, and real photos. The system is described as a serious multi-step AI pipeline rather than a single prompt. A free plan is available that delivers a fully generated site. Brila launched on Product Hunt in April 2026 and ranked #1 for the day, week, and month.
