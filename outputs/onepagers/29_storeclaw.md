## In einem Satz
AI commerce platform with agents that know how to sell, helping e-commerce store owners grow profits with less effort.

## Was sie machen
StoreClaw is an AI commerce platform featuring a collection of agents purpose-built for e-commerce selling. It connects to existing stores (Shopify, WooCommerce, Amazon, eBay) via MCP and autonomously executes tasks — including bulk-editing meta descriptions, adding alt text, generating backlinks, and tweaking product copy — after receiving user approval. StoreClaw studies store numbers, current sales figures, and growth trajectory to offer proactive, actionable suggestions. It comes with 30+ preloaded skills and integrates with major growth channels. Users can query StoreClaw about their business performance at any time. It is positioned not as a chatbot but as an agentic system that directly executes commerce operations on behalf of the merchant.
