## Was sie machen
PollyReach is an AI voice agent platform that gives users a real phone number and a natural-sounding AI voice to handle phone calls. Users can instruct their agent (called "Polly") to make outbound calls — finding the number, dialing, navigating IVR menus, handling conversations, and returning a summary, recording, and transcript. Polly also answers inbound calls 24/7, screens spam, takes messages, tags priority, and sends summaries. It supports 50+ languages and is designed for both individuals and businesses (e.g., property managers handling tenant calls). Unlike enterprise-focused AI phone tools, PollyReach is built for everyday consumers and small businesses.
