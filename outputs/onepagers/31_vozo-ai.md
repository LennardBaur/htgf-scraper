## Was sie machen
Vozo AI delivers complete video translation across every layer — voice dubbing, subtitles, lip-sync, and on-screen text. Unlike traditional dubbing tools, Vozo translates every layer of a video while keeping speech natural, lips perfectly synced, and visuals consistent. The platform enables creators and businesses to turn a single video into multilingual versions that look and feel native, without needing to recreate visuals or manually re-edit content.
