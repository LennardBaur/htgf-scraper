## In einem Satz

## Was sie machen
Chronicle is an AI presentation platform that combines AI generation with powerful editing to create stunning presentations. Users can start from a prompt, notes, or a raw idea and generate beautiful, on-brand slides in minutes. The tool offers full editorial control and supports export to PPT, PDF, and other formats. No design skills are required. Chronicle launched its initial product in March 2023, followed by "Cursor for Slides" in June 2025, and Chronicle 2.0 in March 2026.
