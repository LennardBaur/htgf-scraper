## In einem Satz
AI meeting notetaker that captures, transcribes, and surfaces knowledge from your meetings — bot-free or with a bot, across Zoom, Meet, and Teams.

## Was sie machen
Fathom is an AI-powered meeting notetaker that works across Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams. It captures meetings in bot or bot-free modes, generates automatic notes and summaries, and allows users to ask questions across their entire meeting history with instant, cited answers. Fathom 3.0 introduced bot-free recording, integration with ChatGPT and Claude, CRM connectivity, and support for desktop, mobile, and in-person meetings. The product's core value proposition is unlocking the knowledge inside meetings, not just producing notes. Fathom is rated #1 AI Notetaker on G2 and has received strong community recognition on Product Hunt, including a 2021 Golden Kitty Award, #1 Product of the Day (December 2021), and #1 Product of the Week and #2 Product of the Month for April 2026 with its 3.0 launch.
